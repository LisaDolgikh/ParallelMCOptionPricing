Detailed Description

"Golden Source" pricing engine using the Black-Scholes-Merton formula.

Used for validating Monte Carlo results. Implements exact solutions for European options:

\[ C(S, t) = S N(d_1) - K e^{-rT} N(d_2) \]

where \( N(x) \) is the CDF of the standard normal distribution.
